Quick Start Guide

Stadium Weather provides real-time weather information for football stadiums. Here's how to get started:

  • Install the app from your device's app store
  • Choose between NFL or NCAA football
  • Select your team to view stadium weather
  • Toggle temperature units using the settings menu

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is weather data updated?

Weather data is refreshed every 30 minutes to ensure accuracy.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, previously loaded data is cached for offline viewing.

How do I change temperature units?

Access settings through the gear icon to toggle between Fahrenheit and Celsius.

Troubleshooting

Weather Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the app
  • Clear app cache and try again

Display Issues

  • Ensure your app is updated to the latest version
  • Try toggling dark mode
  • Restart the app
