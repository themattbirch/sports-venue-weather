Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I seeing "No data available"?

This could occur if the stadium data cache is cleared. Try refreshing the extension or reselecting a stadium.

How do I switch temperature units?

Go to the settings menu and select either Fahrenheit (°F) or Celsius (°C) as your preferred unit.

Why does the extension not work offline?

Offline functionality relies on cached data. Ensure you have viewed the stadium's weather while online.

Troubleshooting

Extension not loading

Ensure you have the latest version installed and try restarting your browser.

Weather data not updating

Check your internet connection or refresh the extension from the settings menu.

Still not working?

Clear the extension cache by going to Chrome settings > Extensions > Football Stadium Weather > Remove and reinstall.
